How We Work

Your call workflow,
explained simply.

A walkthrough of how Intake360 handles every call type on behalf of HHJ Trial Attorneys — and how that data reaches your Clio account.
24/7
Live coverage
When HHJ's phones are busy or after hours, calls route directly to Intake360. Every call is answered and handled according to your firm's guidelines.
1
Trigger
Call routes to Intake360
HHJ's call tree forwards missed or overflow calls to our dedicated Intake360 line. No action is needed from your team.
2
Call Answered
Call answered with your firm's profile ready
Before speaking, the agent has your firm's profile, case criteria, and routing instructions on screen — so every call is handled consistently from the first word.
Call type determined on the phone
Path A — Existing client
Current client calling in

Outcome: Email summary sent to HHJ team.
Path B — New potential client
New lead calling in
Agent runs full intake — name, phone, email, and case facts — and qualifies the call against HHJ's criteria in real time.
Outcome: Signed or escalated.
3
New Lead — Qualification
Does the case meet HHJ's criteria?
Clear cases are signed immediately. High-value or borderline cases are escalated before any agreement is sent — your criteria are always the deciding factor.
Clear case → agreement sent Borderline → escalated to David
4
Outcome
Agreement sent — followed up until signed
Once an agreement is out, Intake360 monitors the signature status and follows up via SMS and voicemail until the client signs.

5
Clio Grow
Call data lands in your Clio inbox
For every completed new-lead intake, one record is posted to your Clio Grow inbox automatically. Existing client calls are handled by email — no extra records created.
New signed leads → posted to Clio Existing clients → email to team only
Note on how this reaches Clio: Intake360 makes up to 5 attempts per lead. Only the final result is sent to Clio — not each attempt — so your inbox stays clean and free of duplicates.

Intake360 connects to Clio Grow using your Lead Inbox Token. Once connected, completed intake records are sent to your Clio inbox automatically after every qualifying call.
How it works
1
Agent completes a call intake
When a new lead call results in a Conversion or Evaluation disposition, the call data is packaged automatically within Intake360's system.
2
One record is sent to Clio Grow
A single lead record is posted to your Clio Grow inbox using your Lead Inbox Token. No developer setup is needed on HHJ's end beyond sharing the token.
3
Your team is notified in Clio
The lead appears in your Clio Grow inbox labeled as "Intake360 – Answering Service" so your team can distinguish it from other sources and action it right away.
4
Attempts without contact are not sent
Unsuccessful outbound attempts and existing client calls are handled inside Intake360 only. Nothing is posted to Clio for those — your inbox stays clean.
